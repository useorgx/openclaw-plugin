## Core Concept: OrgX Agents Are Spirits

OrgX agents are not creatures, not robots, not people. They are **organizational spirits** — abstract intelligences that embody a function (orchestration, oversight, execution, design, routing, etc).

In their **base form**, they are ethereal. Energy. Geometry. Force fields with intent. They exist in the OrgX platform as pure organizational logic.

When they **manifest in a client**, they take on the visual language of that world.

### The Three Layers

| Layer | What it is | Visual style | Where it appears |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Spirit Form** (base) | The OrgX identity. Abstract, ethereal, energy-based. | Glowing geometry, constellation patterns, OrgX teal/green-gold energy, no fixed physical shape | OrgX brand materials, platform marketing, the "true self" |
| **Human Interface** | How spirits present when talking directly to users. | The existing human portraits work here — they're the "face" the spirit wears for human comfort | OrgX dashboard, notifications, decision prompts |
| **Client Manifestation** | How spirits appear when they enter a client's world. | Adapts to the client's visual DNA. In OpenClaw = marine-mechanical hybrids. | Crossover marketing, plugin branding, client-specific content |

### Why This Is Better

1. **Platform-agnostic.** OrgX can partner with any client and the spirits just manifest differently. The brand scales infinitely.
2. **Richer lore.** "Ancient organizational spirits that take form in whatever system they inhabit" is a world, not a gimmick.
3. **Makes the existing human assets useful.** Those portraits aren't wrong — they're just one layer. The human face is what the spirit shows you when it needs your approval.
4. **The crossover IS the story.** When OrgX spirits enter the OpenClaw world, they crystallize into marine-mech forms. That transformation is the visual hook for this launch.
5. **Future-proof.** Next client partnership = new manifestation aesthetic. Same spirits, new forms. Content machine.

## The Spirit Forms (Base OrgX Identity)

Each spirit has a **core geometry** and **energy signature** that stays consistent across all manifestations.

| Role | Spirit Name | Core Geometry | Energy Color | Essence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Launch Captain** | **The Sequencer** | Spiral helix — forward motion, ordered progression | Gold / amber pulse | "What's next?" — sets missions, sequencing, momentum |
| **Control Tower** | **The Witness** | Radial eye / panopticon — sees everything at once | Teal / steady glow | "What's happening?" — status, progress, blockers, truth |
| **Engineering Autopilot** | **The Executor** | Arrow / bolt — directed force, precision | Electric green / snap-flash | "Ship it." — turns tasks into done with quality gates |
| **Design Codex** | **The Shaper** | Morphing polygon — constantly resolving into form | Purple / holographic shimmer | "Make it real." — converts intent into crisp output |
| **Product Orchestrator** | **The Weaver** | Web / network graph — connecting everything | Warm amber / threading light | "It all connects." — goals into backlog, decisions |
| **Pipeline Intelligence** | **The Watcher** | Lens / radar sweep — scanning, measuring | Cyan / scanning beam | "What does it cost?" — routes models, watches budget |

### Visual Style for Spirit Forms
```
Abstract energy being, geometric core, no fixed physical shape,
constellation-like particle system, glowing from within,
OrgX teal-to-gold gradient energy, dark void background,
ethereal, ancient, purposeful, organizational intelligence,
clean silhouette, minimal but potent
```

### What Spirit Forms Are NOT
- Not ghosts (no horror/death connotation)
- Not cute mascots (too lightweight)
- Not generic orbs of light (too vague)
- They should feel like **forces** — organizational gravity, structural intelligence, something you'd want on your side

## The OpenClaw Hero: Soft-Shell to Ironclaw

The main character — the OpenClaw bot itself — also gets the spirit treatment.

### Before: Soft-Shell
- Raw red lobster-bot. Exposed joints. Sparking wires. Eyes glowing but unfocused. Multiple limbs doing too many things.
- No spirits. No armor. Running "bareback."
- Represents: powerful but chaotic.

### The Summoning
- OpenClaw calls for help. The OrgX spirits arrive — abstract energy forms swirling around the claw-bot.

### After: Ironclaw
- Same lobster-bot, now with OrgX teal/green armor that looks alive — bioluminescent circuit patterns where the spirits flow.
- HUD visor showing Mission Control hierarchy.
- The crew surrounds it in their manifested forms.
- Represents: harnessed power. "Press continue."
